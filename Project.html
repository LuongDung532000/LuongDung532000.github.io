Project



LD Italian Sofa


LD was formed in 1995 with the mission to bring contemporary design furniture collections, outstanding quality from Italy, Germany, Japan at the most reasonable price. Products from sofas, dining tables to decorative flower vases, etc. are all designed synchronously by Designers from Italy and Australia, creating the most complete and sophisticated living spaces..










Renew

E-commerce application to buy products used fashion


Nowadays, the clothing industry is considered as one of the most significant contributor to environmental & human damage. However, good news is sustainable fashion has a system which works without leaving a negative footprint in order to minimize the environmental impact. ReNew is created with an aim to spread out this concept more widely by providing the young (18-30 years old) a platform to easily buy and sell used items.
